# SEO Strategy Template

## Purpose

This template provides a standardized structure for developing comprehensive SEO strategies that align business objectives with sustainable organic growth, competitive positioning, and AI Search readiness.

The objective is to transform SEO insights into measurable business strategies with clear implementation priorities.

This template should be used for all strategic SEO planning to ensure consistency, business alignment, and execution readiness.
