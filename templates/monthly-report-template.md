# Monthly SEO Report Template

## Purpose

This template provides a standardized structure for presenting monthly SEO performance, business impact, implementation progress, AI Search visibility, and future priorities.

The objective is to communicate measurable business outcomes while enabling stakeholders to track progress and make informed strategic decisions.

Every completed Monthly SEO Report should include:

# Template Usage Notes

This template should be used for all recurring SEO performance reporting to ensure consistency, executive readability, and strategic alignment.

Every monthly report should remain evidence-based, business-focused, measurable, and aligned with long-term organic growth objectives.
