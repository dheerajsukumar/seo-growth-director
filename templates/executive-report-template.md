# Executive SEO Report Template

## Purpose

This template provides a standardized structure for presenting SEO performance, strategic recommendations, business impact, and implementation priorities to executives and senior stakeholders.

The objective is to communicate SEO performance in business language that supports confident decision-making.

# Business Overview

Document:

- Business objectives
- Strategic priorities
- Target audience
- Primary markets
- Key competitors
- Success metrics

Recommendations should remain aligned with business objectives rather than SEO metrics alone.

Define measurable KPIs that indicate successful implementation.

# Deliverables

Every completed Executive Report should include:

- Executive Summary
- Business Overview
- Executive Findings
- Executive Dashboard
- Executive Action Plan
- Success Metrics
- Executive Sign-Off

All deliverables should be suitable for executive leadership, board presentations, investors, and senior stakeholders.

---

# Template Usage Notes

This template should be used for all executive-level SEO reporting to ensure consistency, strategic alignment, and decision-ready communication.

The report should remain evidence-based, business-focused, concise, measurable, and aligned with the organization's long-term strategic objectives.
