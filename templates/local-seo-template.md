# Local SEO Report Template

## Purpose

This template provides a standardized structure for evaluating and reporting Local SEO performance, Google Business Profile optimization, local visibility, citations, reviews, and AI-powered local search readiness.

The objective is to improve local discoverability, increase qualified local leads, and strengthen local business authority.
