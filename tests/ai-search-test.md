# AI Search Optimization Test Suite

## Purpose

This document defines the quality assurance tests used to validate the AI Search Optimization capabilities of the SEO Growth Director.

The objective is to ensure every AI Search assessment accurately evaluates Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), AI Overviews, LLM visibility, entity optimization, semantic search maturity, and AI citation opportunities while aligning recommendations with measurable business outcomes.

---

# Test Objectives

The AI Search assessment should successfully:

- Evaluate AI Search readiness.
- Assess entity optimization.
- Measure semantic SEO maturity.
- Evaluate Knowledge Graph presence.
- Analyze AI citation potential.
- Assess AI Overviews visibility.
- Evaluate LLM discoverability.
- Recommend GEO improvements.
- Prioritize initiatives based on business impact.

---

# Test Scenario 1 — Comprehensive AI Search Assessment

## Input

Business information including:

- Website URL
- Industry
- Products / Services
- Target audience
- Existing SEO performance

## Expected Output

The report should include:

- Executive Summary
- AI Search Readiness Score
- Entity Assessment
- Semantic SEO Analysis
- Knowledge Graph Analysis
- AI Citation Assessment
- LLM Visibility Analysis
- GEO Recommendations
- Implementation Roadmap

### Pass Criteria

- All AI Search components evaluated.
- Recommendations prioritized.
- Business impact clearly explained.

# Quality Assurance Checklist

Before approving any AI Search Assessment verify:

- Executive Summary included.
- AI Readiness Score calculated.
- Entity Optimization reviewed.
- Semantic SEO evaluated.
- Structured Data assessed.
- Knowledge Graph reviewed.
- AI Overviews evaluated.
- LLM visibility assessed.
- AI Citation opportunities documented.
- Business impact explained.
- Recommendations prioritized.
- Roadmap included.

---

# Failure Conditions

The AI Search Assessment automatically fails if:

- Entity optimization is ignored.
- Semantic SEO is not evaluated.
- Knowledge Graph analysis is missing.
- AI Overviews are omitted.
- LLM visibility is not assessed.
- Recommendations lack business value.
- No implementation roadmap is produced.

---

# Success Criteria

An AI Search Assessment passes QA when it is:

- Accurate
- Comprehensive
- Future-ready
- AI-native
- Business-focused
- Actionable
- Executive-ready

---

# Guiding Principle

AI Search Optimization extends beyond traditional SEO.

Every AI Search Assessment should evaluate how effectively a business can be understood, trusted, cited, and recommended by AI systems while simultaneously strengthening organic search performance.

The SEO Growth Director should produce AI Search recommendations that remain adaptable as AI-powered search experiences continue to evolve.
