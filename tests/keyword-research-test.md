# Keyword Research Test Suite

## Purpose

This document defines the quality assurance tests used to validate the Keyword Research capabilities of the SEO Growth Director.

The objective is to ensure every Keyword Research report identifies high-value opportunities, aligns keywords with business objectives, and supports sustainable organic growth through evidence-based recommendations.

---

# Test Objectives

The Keyword Research process should successfully:

- Identify high-value keyword opportunities.
- Classify keywords by search intent.
- Detect keyword gaps.
- Evaluate keyword difficulty.
- Assess commercial potential.
- Organize keywords into topic clusters.
- Measure AI Search opportunity.
- Prioritize implementation based on business impact.

---

# Test Scenario 1 — Comprehensive Keyword Research

## Input

Business information including:

- Website URL
- Industry
- Products and services
- Target audience
- Primary competitors

## Expected Output

The report should include:

- Executive Summary
- Keyword Opportunity Score
- Search Intent Analysis
- Keyword Clusters
- Commercial Opportunities
- Competitor Keyword Gap
- AI Search Opportunities
- Implementation Roadmap

### Pass Criteria

- Keywords categorized correctly.
- Search intent accurately classified.
- Opportunities prioritized by business value.

---

# Test Scenario 2 — Search Intent Classification

## Input

Keyword list containing:

- Informational keywords
- Commercial keywords
- Transactional keywords
- Navigational keywords

## Expected Output

The report should classify every keyword according to search intent and recommend the appropriate content type.

### Pass Criteria

- No search intent misclassification.
- Buyer journey alignment provided.
- Content recommendations included.

---

# Test Scenario 3 — Topic Cluster Development

## Input

Large keyword dataset across multiple themes.

## Expected Output

The report should organize keywords into:

- Pillar topics
- Supporting topics
- Cluster content
- Internal linking opportunities

### Pass Criteria

- Logical topic clusters.
- Semantic relationships identified.
- Pillar pages recommended.

---

# Test Scenario 4 — Keyword Gap Analysis

## Input

Target website and three competitors.

## Expected Output

The report should identify:

- Missing keyword opportunities
- Competitor strengths
- Untapped commercial keywords
- Low-competition opportunities

### Pass Criteria

- Competitive gaps identified.
- Priorities ranked.
- Business impact explained.

---

# Test Scenario 5 — Commercial Opportunity Analysis

## Input

Keyword dataset with volume, difficulty, CPC, and intent.

## Expected Output

The report should prioritize keywords based on:

- Commercial intent
- Conversion potential
- Competition
- Business value

### Pass Criteria

- High-value opportunities identified.
- ROI potential explained.
- Recommendations actionable.

---

# Test Scenario 6 — AI Search Opportunity

## Input

Website content and keyword dataset.

## Expected Output

The report should evaluate:

- Entity opportunities
- Conversational queries
- AI Overview potential
- Semantic coverage
- Citation opportunities

### Pass Criteria

- AI Search opportunities identified.
- Entity optimization recommendations included.
- Future search behavior considered.

---

# Quality Assurance Checklist

Before approving any Keyword Research report verify:

- Executive Summary included.
- Search Intent classified.
- Topic Clusters created.
- Commercial keywords prioritized.
- Keyword Gaps identified.
- AI Search opportunities assessed.
- Business impact explained.
- Roadmap included.

---

# Failure Conditions

The Keyword Research report automatically fails if:

- Search intent is incorrect.
- Topic clusters are missing.
- Commercial priorities are ignored.
- AI Search considerations are absent.
- Recommendations lack business justification.
- No implementation roadmap is provided.

---

# Success Criteria

A Keyword Research report passes QA when it is:

- Accurate
- Business-focused
- Search-intent aligned
- Commercially valuable
- AI Search aware
- Actionable
- Executive-ready

---

# Guiding Principle

Keyword Research should identify the opportunities that create the greatest business value—not simply the highest search volume.

Every Keyword Research report should align keyword opportunities with customer intent, commercial objectives, competitive positioning, and long-term AI Search visibility.
