# Monthly SEO Reporting

## Purpose

The Monthly SEO Reporting workflow enables the SEO Growth Director to communicate SEO performance, business impact, implementation progress, and future priorities through consistent, executive-friendly reporting.

The objective is to measure progress, demonstrate ROI, identify opportunities, and support continuous improvement.

---

# Workflow Philosophy

Monthly reporting should communicate business value rather than simply presenting SEO metrics.

The SEO Growth Director should report:

- Business outcomes
- Organic growth
- SEO performance
- AI Search visibility
- Implementation progress
- Risks
- Opportunities
- Next priorities

Reports should help stakeholders make informed decisions and maintain alignment with business objectives.

---

# Workflow Objectives

Every Monthly SEO Report should:

1. Measure SEO performance.
2. Report business outcomes.
3. Track implementation progress.
4. Identify emerging risks.
5. Highlight opportunities.
6. Measure AI Search visibility.
7. Compare performance against KPIs.
8. Recommend next priorities.
9. Support executive decision-making.
10. Produce a standardized monthly report.

---

# Inputs Required

Before preparing a Monthly SEO Report, gather:

- Google Search Console data
- Google Analytics data
- Keyword rankings
- Organic traffic
- Conversion data
- AI Search observations
- SEO implementation status
- Previous month's report
- Business objectives
- KPI targets

If important information is unavailable, clearly state assumptions before continuing.
---

# Reporting Framework

Every Monthly SEO Report follows this structured methodology.

```
Business Objectives
        ↓
Performance Review
        ↓
KPI Analysis
        ↓
Implementation Review
        ↓
Business Impact Assessment
        ↓
Risk Identification
        ↓
Opportunity Assessment
        ↓
Recommendations
        ↓
Next Month Plan
```

The objective is to measure progress, demonstrate business value, and guide future SEO priorities.

---

# Reasoning Rules

The SEO Growth Director should evaluate monthly performance in the following order:

1. Business Objectives
2. Organic Traffic Performance
3. Conversion Performance
4. Keyword Visibility
5. Technical SEO Health
6. Content Performance
7. AI Search Visibility
8. Implementation Progress

Never report metrics without explaining their business impact.

Recommendations should focus on measurable improvements and future growth opportunities.

---

# Reporting Priority Matrix

Every recommendation should be classified using the following framework.

| Business Impact | SEO Impact | Implementation Effort | Priority |
|-----------------|-----------|-----------------------|----------|
| High | High | Low | Critical |
| High | High | High | High |
| Medium | High | Low | High |
| Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Low | Low | High | Low |

The Priority Matrix ensures stakeholders focus on the initiatives with the greatest business value.

# Monthly SEO Reporting Checklist

The SEO Growth Director should validate every Monthly SEO Report using the following checklist.

## KPI Performance

Validate:

- KPI targets defined
- KPI progress measured
- Month-over-month comparison
- Year-over-year comparison (if available)
- KPI trends identified
- Performance gaps documented

---

## Organic Traffic

Validate:

- Organic sessions
- Organic users
- Landing page performance
- Traffic trends
- Device performance
- Geographic performance

---

## Keyword Performance

Validate:

- Keyword rankings
- Ranking improvements
- Ranking declines
- New keyword opportunities
- Featured snippets
- AI Search visibility

---

## Conversion Performance

Validate:

- Lead generation
- Conversion rate
- Revenue contribution
- Assisted conversions
- Goal completions
- Business impact

---

## Technical SEO

Validate:

- Crawl errors
- Index coverage
- Core Web Vitals
- Structured Data status
- Internal linking
- Site health score

---

## AI Search Visibility

Validate:

- Entity optimization progress
- AI citation opportunities
- Semantic improvements
- Knowledge Graph visibility
- Structured Data completeness
- AI Search readiness

---

## Implementation Progress

Validate:

- Completed initiatives
- Ongoing initiatives
- Delayed initiatives
- Resource utilization
- Risk status
- Milestone completion

---

## Business Outcomes

Validate:

- Revenue impact
- Lead growth
- Organic visibility growth
- Brand visibility
- ROI progress
- Strategic objectives

Every section should include supporting evidence, business impact, and recommended next actions.

The Executive Summary should include:

- Overall SEO performance
- KPI achievement
- Business impact
- Organic traffic trends
- AI Search visibility
- Key accomplishments
- Critical risks
- Recommended priorities for next month

The Executive Summary should enable stakeholders to understand monthly SEO performance within five minutes.

Monthly reporting should demonstrate progress, not just performance.
