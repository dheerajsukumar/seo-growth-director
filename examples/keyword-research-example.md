# Keyword Research Example

## Purpose

This document demonstrates what an excellent Keyword Research report should look like when generated by the SEO Growth Director.

It serves as the reference implementation for:

- Keyword discovery
- Search intent mapping
- Topic clustering
- Business prioritization
- Opportunity analysis
- Executive presentation

Every future Keyword Research report should aim to meet or exceed the quality demonstrated in this example.

# Keyword Research Findings

## Opportunity 1

### Category

Commercial Keywords

### Priority

Critical

### Evidence

- High search volume for commercial-intent keywords
- Low current ranking visibility
- Strong conversion potential
- Competitors dominate Page 1

### Root Cause

The website focuses primarily on informational content and lacks optimized commercial landing pages.

### Business Impact

- Lost qualified leads
- Lower conversion potential
- Reduced market visibility
- Competitive disadvantage

### Recommendation

Develop dedicated commercial landing pages targeting high-intent keywords and strengthen supporting internal links.

### Expected Outcome

- Higher qualified traffic
- Increased conversions
- Improved commercial rankings

---

## Opportunity 2

### Category

Topic Clusters

### Priority

High

### Evidence

- Isolated blog content
- Weak topical relationships
- Limited supporting articles
- Incomplete keyword coverage

### Root Cause

Content has been published without a structured topic cluster strategy.

### Business Impact

- Lower topical authority
- Reduced organic visibility
- Weaker AI Search recognition
- Missed long-tail opportunities

### Recommendation

Build comprehensive topic clusters with pillar pages and supporting content mapped to the buyer journey.

### Expected Outcome

- Stronger topical authority
- Improved keyword coverage
- Better AI Search visibility
- Sustainable organic growth

# Key Takeaways

This example demonstrates the expected quality standard for every Keyword Research report produced by the SEO Growth Director.

Every keyword research report should:

- Present evidence before conclusions.
- Align keyword opportunities with business objectives.
- Prioritize recommendations by business impact.
- Include measurable success metrics.
- Provide a phased implementation roadmap.
- Support executive decision-making while remaining actionable for SEO and content teams.
