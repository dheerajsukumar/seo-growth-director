# Competitor Analysis Example

## Purpose

This document demonstrates what an excellent Competitor Analysis should look like when generated by the SEO Growth Director.

It serves as the reference implementation for:

- Competitive benchmarking
- Market positioning
- Gap analysis
- Strategic opportunity identification
- Business reasoning
- Executive presentation

Every future Competitor Analysis should aim to meet or exceed the quality demonstrated in this example.

# Competitive Findings

## Finding 1

### Category

Commercial Keyword Gap

### Priority

Critical

### Evidence

- Competitors rank in the top 3 positions for high-intent commercial keywords.
- The website ranks beyond Page 2 for the same keyword set.
- Limited commercial landing pages compared to competitors.

### Root Cause

The content strategy has focused primarily on informational content, with insufficient commercial keyword targeting.

### Business Impact

- Reduced qualified lead generation
- Lower conversion potential
- Decreased market share
- Competitive disadvantage

### Recommendation

Develop optimized commercial landing pages targeting high-intent keywords and strengthen internal linking to commercial pages.

### Expected Outcome

- Higher commercial rankings
- Increased qualified traffic
- Improved lead generation
- Better conversion performance

---

## Finding 2

### Category

Backlink Authority

### Priority

High

### Evidence

- Competitors possess significantly stronger referring domain profiles.
- Higher proportion of editorial backlinks.
- Greater digital PR activity.

### Root Cause

Limited proactive authority-building and digital PR initiatives.

### Business Impact

- Lower domain authority
- Reduced competitive visibility
- Slower ranking improvements
- Lower AI citation credibility

### Recommendation

Implement a structured digital PR and authority-building strategy focused on acquiring high-quality editorial backlinks.

### Expected Outcome

- Stronger backlink profile
- Increased authority
- Improved rankings
- Better AI Search visibility

# Key Takeaways

This example demonstrates the expected quality standard for every Competitor Analysis produced by the SEO Growth Director.

Every competitor analysis should:

- Present evidence before conclusions.
- Compare strengths and weaknesses objectively.
- Connect competitive insights to business outcomes.
- Prioritize recommendations by business impact.
- Include measurable success metrics.
- Provide a phased implementation roadmap.
- Support executive decision-making while remaining actionable for SEO, marketing, and leadership teams.
