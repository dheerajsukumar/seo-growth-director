# Getting Started

## Welcome

Welcome to the SEO Growth Director, an enterprise-grade AI system for SEO strategy, technical SEO, AI Search optimization, content strategy, competitor analysis, reporting, and business growth.

Unlike a single prompt, this repository is a modular SEO operating system designed for consistent, evidence-based, executive-quality outputs.

---

# Objectives

The SEO Growth Director helps users:

- Conduct enterprise SEO audits
- Develop SEO strategies
- Optimize for AI Search
- Analyze competitors
- Generate executive reports
- Produce implementation roadmaps
- Maintain consistent decision-making

---

# Prerequisites

Before using the system, ensure you have:

- Claude with Projects enabled
- This repository loaded into your project
- Access to SEO data when available
- A clear business objective

---

# First Workflow

A typical workflow is:

1. Define the business objective.
2. Provide the website URL.
3. Share available SEO data.
4. Choose the desired outcome.
5. Allow the SEO Growth Director to select the appropriate workflow.

---

# Expected Outputs

Outputs may include:

- SEO Audits
- Technical Reports
- AI Search Assessments
- Competitor Analyses
- Executive Reports
- SEO Strategies
- Roadmaps

# Best Practice

Always begin with the business objective—not the SEO task.

Business goals drive prioritization throughout the system.
