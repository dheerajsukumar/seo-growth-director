# Workflow Guide

## Purpose

This document describes how workflows are selected and executed inside the SEO Growth Director.

---

# Workflow Selection

The system automatically selects the most appropriate workflow based on user intent.

Supported workflows include:

- Technical SEO Audit
- Content Audit
- Keyword Research
- Competitor Analysis
- Local SEO
- Ecommerce SEO
- AI Search Assessment
- Website Migration
- Executive Reporting
- SEO Strategy

---

# Standard Workflow

Every workflow follows this sequence:

Business Objective

↓

Evidence Collection

↓

Situation Analysis

↓

Business Impact Assessment

↓

Recommendation Prioritization

↓

Implementation Planning

↓

Success Metrics

↓

Executive Output

---

# Workflow Principles

Every workflow should:

- Remain evidence-based.
- Explain business impact.
- Prioritize actions.
- Include measurable outcomes.
- Align with executive decision-making.

---

# Workflow Consistency

Regardless of workflow type, every deliverable should:

- Maintain consistent terminology.
- Follow standard templates.
- Use shared reasoning frameworks.
- Produce executive-ready communication.
