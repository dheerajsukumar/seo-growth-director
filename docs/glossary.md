# Glossary

## AI Search

AI-powered search experiences that generate synthesized answers using large language models and search systems.

---

## AI Citation

A reference made by an AI system to a website or brand while generating responses.

---

## Entity

A uniquely identifiable person, organization, place, product, or concept recognized by search engines.

---

## GEO

Generative Engine Optimization.

The practice of improving visibility within AI-powered search experiences.

---

## Knowledge Graph

A structured representation of entities and their relationships.

---

## LLM

Large Language Model.

Examples include Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and other AI systems.

---

## Semantic SEO

Optimizing content based on meaning, relationships, and context rather than isolated keywords.

---

## Structured Data

Machine-readable markup that helps search engines understand webpage content.

---

## Topical Authority

The perceived expertise of a website within a subject area based on comprehensive, interconnected content.

---

## Search Intent

The underlying purpose behind a user's search query.

Common categories include:

- Informational
- Navigational
- Commercial
- Transactional

---

## E-E-A-T

Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness.

---

## Core Web Vitals

Google's user experience metrics:

- Largest Contentful Paint (LCP)
- Interaction to Next Paint (INP)
- Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS)

---

## Organic Visibility

The extent to which a website appears in unpaid search results.

---

## Technical SEO

Optimization focused on crawlability, indexability, performance, structured data, and website architecture.

---

## SEO Roadmap

A prioritized implementation plan aligning SEO initiatives with business objectives.
