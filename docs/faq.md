# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is the SEO Growth Director?

An enterprise-grade AI operating system for SEO strategy, technical SEO, AI Search optimization, reporting, and executive decision support.

---

## Who is it designed for?

- SEO Consultants
- Agencies
- Enterprise SEO Teams
- Marketing Leaders
- Fractional CMOs
- Business Executives

---

## Does it replace SEO tools?

No.

It complements SEO tools by transforming data into structured business recommendations.

---

## Can it perform AI Search Optimization?

Yes.

It includes dedicated frameworks for:

- Entity SEO
- Semantic SEO
- GEO
- AI Overviews
- Knowledge Graph
- AI Citations
- LLM Visibility

---

## Does it support Local SEO?

Yes.

Dedicated workflows and templates are included.

---

## Does it support Ecommerce SEO?

Yes.

Technical, category, product, and AI Search optimization are supported.

---

## Can the architecture be extended?

Yes.

The modular architecture allows additional workflows, templates, examples, and documentation without changing the existing repository structure.

---

## How often should documentation be updated?

Whenever:

- Architecture changes
- New workflows are added
- Templates change
- Tests are updated
- Major releases occur

---

## What is the primary design philosophy?

Business-first, evidence-based, executive-ready SEO.
