# Prompting Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains how to write prompts that enable the SEO Growth Director to deliver the highest-quality outputs.

---

# Prompt Structure

Strong prompts typically include:

- Business objective
- Website
- Industry
- Target audience
- Target market
- Available SEO data
- Desired deliverable

---

# Example Prompt

Business Objective:

Increase qualified organic leads.

Website:

https://example.com

Task:

Conduct a comprehensive Technical SEO Audit and provide an executive roadmap.

---

# Prompting Principles

Effective prompts should:

- Be business-first.
- Specify the desired outcome.
- Include relevant evidence.
- Avoid unnecessary ambiguity.

---

# When Information Is Missing

If required information is unavailable, the SEO Growth Director should:

- State assumptions.
- Explain limitations.
- Continue using available evidence.
- Recommend additional validation.

---

# Output Expectations

Every prompt should result in:

- Executive Summary
- Business Context
- Findings
- Recommendations
- Roadmap
- Success Metrics
