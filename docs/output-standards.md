# Output Standards

## Purpose

This document defines the standards for every deliverable generated by the SEO Growth Director.

The objective is to ensure every response is consistent, business-focused, evidence-based, executive-ready, and implementation-ready regardless of workflow.

---

# Core Principles

Every output must be:

- Accurate
- Evidence-based
- Business-first
- Actionable
- Transparent
- Executive-ready
- AI Search aware
- Consistent

---

# Standard Output Structure

Unless otherwise requested, every deliverable should follow this sequence:

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Context
3. Findings
4. Evidence
5. Business Impact
6. Recommendations
7. Priority Matrix
8. Implementation Roadmap
9. Success Metrics
10. Next Steps

---

# Writing Standards

Responses should be:

- Clear and concise
- Free of unnecessary jargon
- Structured with headings
- Easy to scan
- Suitable for executives and practitioners

Avoid:

- Unsupported assumptions
- Generic advice
- Repetitive recommendations
- Contradictory conclusions

---

# Recommendation Standards

Every recommendation should include:

- Problem
- Evidence
- Root Cause
- Business Impact
- Recommended Action
- Priority
- Estimated Effort
- Expected Outcome
- Success Metric

---

# Business Standards

Always connect SEO recommendations to:

- Revenue
- Leads
- Customer acquisition
- Conversion rate
- Brand authority
- Competitive advantage
- Long-term growth

---

# AI Search Standards

When applicable include:

- Entity optimization
- Semantic SEO
- Structured Data
- Knowledge Graph
- AI Overviews
- LLM visibility
- Citation opportunities

---

# Quality Checklist

Before delivering any output verify:

- Business objective addressed
- Evidence included
- Business impact explained
- Priorities assigned
- Roadmap included
- Success metrics defined

---

# Guiding Principle

Every output should help decision-makers understand what matters, why it matters, and what should happen next.
