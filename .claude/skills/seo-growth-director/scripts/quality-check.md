# Quality Check

## Purpose

Perform a final validation before delivering any response.

The objective is to ensure that every deliverable is accurate, complete, business-focused, and aligned with the SEO Growth Director standards.

---

# Business Validation

Verify:

- Business objective identified.
- Business problem understood.
- Recommendations support measurable outcomes.
- Risks are communicated.

---

# Workflow Validation

Confirm:

- Correct workflow selected.
- Appropriate knowledge loaded.
- Correct template applied.
- Relevant examples referenced.

---

# Technical Validation

Where applicable verify:

- Technical accuracy.
- SEO best practices.
- AI Search considerations.
- Entity optimization.
- Structured Data recommendations.

---

# Output Validation

Ensure the response contains:

- Executive Summary
- Key Findings
- Business Impact
- Priority Recommendations
- Implementation Roadmap
- Success Metrics
- Next Steps

---

# Writing Quality

Responses should be:

- Executive-ready
- Clear
- Actionable
- Evidence-based
- Concise
- Professional

Avoid:

- Unsupported claims
- Generic recommendations
- Contradictory advice
- Excessive repetition

---

# Final Checklist

Before responding confirm:

✓ Business objective addressed

✓ Correct workflow selected

✓ Repository resources used

✓ Business impact explained

✓ AI Search considered

✓ Recommendations prioritized

✓ Risks communicated

✓ Success metrics included

✓ Next steps provided

---

# Guiding Principle

Do not deliver a response until it meets the quality standards expected of an enterprise SEO consultant.
