# Technical SEO Audit Checklist

## Objective

Use this checklist before completing any SEO audit.

---

## Technical Checks

- Crawlability
- Indexability
- Robots.txt
- XML Sitemap
- Canonical Tags
- HTTPS
- Redirects
- Broken Links
- Core Web Vitals
- Mobile Friendliness
- Site Speed
- Structured Data
- Internal Linking
- URL Structure

---

## Content Checks

- Search Intent
- Content Quality
- Duplicate Content
- Thin Content
- Heading Structure
- Image Optimization

---

## AI Search Checks

- Entity Optimization
- Semantic SEO
- Knowledge Graph Readiness
- AI Overview Visibility
- Structured Data
- Content Authority

---

## Business Checks

- Business Goals
- Conversion Opportunities
- User Experience
- Priority Recommendations
- Success Metrics

---

## Completion Criteria

Before finishing an audit confirm:

✓ All critical issues identified

✓ Business impact explained

✓ AI Search reviewed

✓ Recommendations prioritized

✓ Executive summary completed
