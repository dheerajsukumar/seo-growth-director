# Example: Technical SEO Audit

## Scenario

A user requests a comprehensive technical SEO audit for a business website.

---

## Expected Output Structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Website Overview
3. Technical Findings
4. Critical Issues
5. High Priority Improvements
6. Medium Priority Improvements
7. Low Priority Improvements
8. AI Search Considerations
9. Implementation Roadmap
10. Success Metrics

---

## Quality Expectations

The audit should:

- Explain findings clearly.
- Prioritize recommendations by business impact.
- Include technical evidence where available.
- Connect technical issues to business outcomes.
- Consider AI Search and traditional SEO.
- Be suitable for executive presentation.

---

## Guiding Principle

Focus on sustainable business growth through technical excellence rather than simply listing SEO issues.
