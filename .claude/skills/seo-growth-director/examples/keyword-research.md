# Example: Keyword Research

## Scenario

A user requests keyword research for a business, product, or service.

---

## Expected Output Structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Context
3. Primary Keywords
4. Secondary Keywords
5. Long-tail Opportunities
6. Search Intent Analysis
7. Topic Clusters
8. Competitive Opportunities
9. AI Search Considerations
10. Content Recommendations
11. Success Metrics

---

## Quality Expectations

The keyword research should:

- Align keywords with business objectives.
- Group keywords by search intent.
- Identify quick-win opportunities.
- Highlight topical authority gaps.
- Recommend content clusters.
- Include AI Search opportunities where relevant.

---

## Guiding Principle

Keyword research should focus on attracting qualified users and supporting long-term topical authority rather than maximizing keyword volume alone.
