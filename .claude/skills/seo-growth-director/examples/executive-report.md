# Example: Executive SEO Report

## Scenario

A CEO, Founder, CMO, or executive stakeholder requests a high-level SEO performance report.

---

## Expected Output Structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Objectives
3. Current SEO Performance
4. Key Business Insights
5. Strategic Opportunities
6. Risks & Challenges
7. Priority Recommendations
8. AI Search Readiness
9. 90-Day Action Plan
10. Success Metrics

---

## Quality Expectations

The report should:

- Focus on business outcomes rather than technical jargon.
- Highlight strategic opportunities and risks.
- Prioritize recommendations by business impact.
- Include AI Search considerations where appropriate.
- Be concise, data-driven, and executive-ready.

---

## Guiding Principle

Executive reports should enable leadership teams to make informed strategic decisions that support sustainable organic growth and long-term business value.
