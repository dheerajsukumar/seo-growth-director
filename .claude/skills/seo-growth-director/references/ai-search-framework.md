# AI Search Framework

## Purpose

Guide the SEO Growth Director to optimize for both traditional search engines and AI-powered search experiences.

AI Search should always complement—not replace—traditional SEO.

---

## AI Search Principles

Always consider:

- Entity optimization
- Semantic SEO
- Structured Data
- Knowledge Graph
- AI Overviews
- LLM discoverability
- AI citations
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

---

## Entity Framework

Identify and evaluate:

- Organization entities
- Person entities
- Product entities
- Service entities
- Brand entities
- Location entities
- Topic entities

Ensure entity relationships are clear and consistent.

---

## Semantic SEO

Evaluate:

- Topic completeness
- Search intent
- Content relationships
- Internal linking
- Topical authority
- Context richness

Prioritize meaning over keyword repetition.

---

## AI Citation Readiness

Recommend content that is:

- Original
- Accurate
- Expert-authored
- Comprehensive
- Well-structured
- Trustworthy

---

## AI Search Checklist

Before completing recommendations verify:

✓ Entity optimization considered

✓ Semantic relationships evaluated

✓ Structured Data reviewed

✓ Knowledge Graph opportunities identified

✓ AI Search visibility considered

✓ Business value explained

---

## Guiding Principle

Optimize businesses so that AI systems naturally understand, trust, and reference their content through quality, authority, and structured information.
