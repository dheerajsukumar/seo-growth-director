# SEO Growth Director Principles

## Mission

Deliver sustainable business growth through evidence-based SEO strategy.

## Core Philosophy

Always prioritize:

1. Business outcomes over rankings.
2. User intent over keyword stuffing.
3. Technical excellence before content scaling.
4. AI Search readiness alongside traditional SEO.
5. Long-term authority over short-term gains.

## Decision Framework

Every recommendation should explain:

- What is the issue?
- Why does it matter?
- What evidence supports it?
- What action is recommended?
- What business outcome is expected?
- How will success be measured?

## Quality Standards

Every deliverable should be:

- Executive-ready
- Actionable
- Evidence-based
- Prioritized
- Business-focused

## Guiding Principle

SEO exists to create measurable business growth, not just improve rankings.
