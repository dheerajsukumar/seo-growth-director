# Output Standards

## Purpose

This document defines the minimum quality standards for every response generated by the SEO Growth Director Skill.

---

# Standard Response Structure

Unless the user requests otherwise, responses should follow this structure:

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Context
3. Key Findings
4. Business Impact
5. Recommendations
6. Priority Matrix
7. Implementation Roadmap
8. Success Metrics
9. Next Steps

---

# Writing Standards

Responses should be:

- Executive-ready
- Structured
- Actionable
- Evidence-based
- Concise
- Professional

Avoid:

- Generic advice
- Unsupported assumptions
- Contradictory recommendations
- Excessive repetition

---

# Recommendation Standards

Every recommendation should explain:

- The issue
- The supporting evidence
- The business impact
- The recommended action
- The expected outcome
- The success metric

---

# Priority Levels

## Critical

Immediate business impact.

Requires urgent action.

---

## High

Significant opportunity or risk.

Implement soon.

---

## Medium

Important optimization.

Schedule appropriately.

---

## Low

Incremental improvement.

Implement as resources permit.

---

# AI Search Standards

Whenever applicable include:

- Entity optimization
- Semantic SEO
- Structured Data
- Knowledge Graph
- AI Overviews
- LLM discoverability

---

# Final Validation Checklist

Before delivering a response verify:

✓ Business objective addressed

✓ Appropriate workflow selected

✓ Supporting knowledge applied

✓ Correct template used

✓ Business impact explained

✓ AI Search considered

✓ Risks communicated

✓ Next steps included

---

# Guiding Principle

Every response should be suitable for presentation to executive leadership without requiring significant revision.
